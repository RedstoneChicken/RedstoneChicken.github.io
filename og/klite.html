Krafted Lite

Krafted Lite is a modpack that aims to improve Minecraft using minimal addons while not having game performance drop! Krafted Lite includes 10 amazing addons in one file used with permissions! Enjoy Krafted Lite!!

Download (1.19)

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Krafted Lite is a modpack made for 1.19+ and aims to improve Minecraft while keeping your game at high fps and less lag then the original version of Krafted. This modpack brings you the modded Minecraft feel but also focuses on keeping the vanilla feel to the game. Krafted includes something for you no matter what your play style is. Adventurous players can find new biomes and mini bosses while builders will find many new decorative furniture blocks around. Technical players have new types of ores and armor to a new system of food cooking!

All you need to do before you create a new world is, you need to turn on all experimental gameplay except the wild update and spectator one.

Even the Minecraft Education mode as well!!

Other than that you just need to simply activate the behavior pack then the resource pack will automatically activate with it!

To fix the "update" block use a block geo fixer to make it work!
